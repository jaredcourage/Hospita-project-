    Surgical Care

    CuraSolace Health Sciences Centre's (CSHSC) surgical program is dedicated to providing outstanding care

    We also provide a range of other care services from prenatal to postpartum and newborn care to ensure you are supported through your journey towards the birth of your new baby. for all residents of Australia & Canada . Our surgeons and operating room staff are specially for all residents of Austrlia and Canada trained and have expertise in eleven distinct areas including General Surgery, Orthopedic Surgery, Plastic Surgery, Neurosurgery, Cardiovascular Surgery, Urology, Gynecology, Ear Nose and Throat, Dental Surgery, Vascular Surgery and Thoracic Surgery.

    As the largest surgical program in the region, you may also be referred to CSH from another hospital for a special surgical procedure that is only available here. Our surgical teams complete the most complex procedures in the southeast region, so you can rest assured that you will be in good hands.
